DumbleDoor

This website basically concentrates on the world's one of the leading problems - Pollution.

The door is named DumbleDoor which is a magical door that opens right into the solutions ;)

The website potrays an animation of a moving car that produces smoke and when a magical wand is moved around it, all the smoke is vanished, just like that and therefore no smoke is entering the air.

Who knew a wand could eradicate air pollution, right? :)
